Introduction

Emerging technologies are transforming by providing innovative solutions. Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, Cyber Security, and Internet of Things (IoT) are among the most important technologies today.

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) enables machines to perform tasks such as learning, reasoning, problem-solving, and decision-making.

Internet of Things

The Internet of Things (IoT) connects smart devices through the Internet, enabling communication and automation.

Cloud

Cloud Computing provides on-demand computing resources including servers, storage, databases, and software over the Internet.

Cyber Security

Cyber Security protects computer systems, networks, and sensitive information from cyber threats and unauthorized access.
